Experienced Professionals

Experienced Professionals play a vital role in managing NNPC’s construction projects and in the day to day operations of NNPC’s assets such as refineries, oil fields and chemical plants. Specific disciplines include project management engineers, asset management and operations engineers and technical support engineers. Responsibilities may include project scoping, cost estimating, detailed engineering design, as well as solving challenges and implementing solutions.
